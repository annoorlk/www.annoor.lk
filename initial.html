Our Sacred Mission

Islam is a vibrant and divinely blessed religion capable of addressing societal needs through time. We established our institution to ensure Islam thrives as a living religion in Sri Lanka.

Founded in the early 1990s at Siyambalagasakotuwa Jummah Masjid, we've grown into a respected institution serving hundreds of students.

Our Serene Campus Location

The college is located approximately 90 km from the nation's capital, Colombo, in the North Western Province.

It is situated in the Kurunegala District, within the Kuliyapitiya electoral division.
